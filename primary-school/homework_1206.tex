\section*{Problem 1}
\textbf{例题：}
计算：


\[
    1 \times 2 + 2 \times 3 + 3 \times 4 + \cdots + 9 \times 10
\]
\textbf{解：} 观察每一项，可以发现：
\[
    \begin{aligned}
        1 \times 2  & = \frac{1}{3}(1 \times 2 \times 3 - 0 \times 1 \times 2)    \\
        2 \times 3  & = \frac{1}{3}(2 \times 3 \times 4 - 1 \times 2 \times 3)    \\
        3 \times 4  & = \frac{1}{3}(3 \times 4 \times 5 - 2 \times 3 \times 4)    \\
                    & \vdots                                                      \\
        9 \times 10 & = \frac{1}{3}(9 \times 10 \times 11 - 8 \times 9 \times 10)
    \end{aligned}
\]
\text{这里应用一个方法，错位相消}
\[
    \begin{aligned}
         = \frac{1}{3}(9 \times 10 \times 11 - 0 \times 1 \times 2) & = \frac{1}{3}(990 - 0) \\
            & = 330
    \end{aligned}
\]
