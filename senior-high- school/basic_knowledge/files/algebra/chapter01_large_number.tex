\textbf{解：}
设 $AD = x$，则 $DB=6-x$，\newline
$\triangle ACD \sim \triangle BDF$，\newline
$\frac{AC}{BD} = \frac{AD}{BF}$，\newline
$\frac{5}{6-x} = \frac{x}{BF}$，\newline
$BF = \frac{6x-x^2}{5}$，\newline
$BF$最大值为 $\frac{9}{5}$。\newline
$\therefore \min CF = \frac{16}{5}$。
